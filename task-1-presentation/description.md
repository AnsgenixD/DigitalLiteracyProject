###Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic###

*Tool used - Canva*

This presentation is a single-paged infographic to help my batchmates better understand what Digital Literacy means and why it matters.

It covers three questions most asked:

1. What Digital Literacy means
2. Safe Internet Practices
3. Professional Online Presence Tips


One thing I learned/found interesting is how each different layout completely changes the style of the output infographic. A roadmap was unsuitable, a heavy-text cluttered presentation was unsuitable, so I used a minimalist and simple presentation to show with added infographics
